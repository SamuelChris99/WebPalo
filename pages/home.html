ABOUT US

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter (FPS) developed by Riot Games. It blends precise gunplay reminiscent of Counter-Strike with unique character abilities similar to Overwatch. The game features 5v5 matches where teams alternate between attacking and defending over multiple rounds. Players choose agents with special abilities that can be used strategically to control the map, counter opponents, or secure objectives. With its emphasis on teamwork, tactical depth, and individual skill, Valorant has become one of the most popular esports titles today.

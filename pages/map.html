Bond

Bond is an iconic Valorant map set in a desert-themed research facility.Unlike other maps, it has no mid, forcing players to choose between Site A and Site B. Its standout feature is the two teleporters, connecting A Short to B Long and B Long to A Lobby, allowing quick rotations but making loud noises that reveal movement. Site A offers access through A Short and Showers, while Site B features B Long for long-range duels and Hookah for close combat. Bind's design emphasizes strategy and quick decision-making, making it a favorite among players.

Hapen

Hapen is a unique Valorant map set in a serene monastery, notable for having three bomb sites: A, B, and C, making it the only map of its kind. This layout forces defenders to spread thin while giving attackers more strategic options. Site A can be accessed via A Long and A Short, requiring utility for a safe push, while Site B, located centrally, often becomes a contested hotspot. Site C features entry through C Long and Garage, favoring long-range engagements. Control of mid is crucial, as it connects all sites and enables quick rotations. Haven's layout rewards coordinated strategies and versatile gameplay, making it a dynamic and challenging map.

Splat

Splat is a Valorant map set in a futuristic urban environment, characterized by its towering skyscrapers and neon-lit aesthetics. The map is known for its verticality and tight choke points, with ropes providing unique mobility options. Split features two bomb sites, A and B, both requiring careful planning and utility usage to execute. Site A is accessible through A Main and A Ramp, with the high ground on Rafters often giving defenders an advantage. Site B can be approached via B Main and Mid Vent, with Heaven offering a strong defensive position. Controlling mid is crucial on Splat, as it opens up opportunities to pressure both sites. With its narrow corridors and vertical gameplay, Split demands precision, coordination, and strategic use of utilities.

fraclure

Fraclure is a uniquely designed Valorant map set in a top-secret Radianite research facility with a split layout that surrounds two bomb sites, A and B. The map's standout feature is its dual attacker spawns, allowing attackers to approach from opposite sides, creating unpredictable strategies. Site A is accessible through A Drop and A Dish, while Site B can be reached via B Arcade and B Main, both offering a mix of open and tight spaces. The central zipline beneath the map adds mobility and strategic depth, enabling quick rotations and surprise plays. Fraclure's design encourages creative strategies, multi-directional attacks, and coordinated team play, making it one of the most innovative maps in Valorant.

icebop

Icebop is a Valorant map set in a snowy, industrial shipping facility located in the Arctic. Known for its verticality and complex layout, Icebop features two bomb sites, A and B, both offering unique challenges. Site A is dominated by a towering platform and a maze of crates, providing opportunities for high-ground engagements and requiring precise utility use to clear angles. Site B features a large container in the center, often serving as a plant spot, with tight chokepoints leading into the site. Ropes and ziplines are key elements, adding mobility and vertical gameplay options. Icebox rewards teams with sharp aim and strong coordination, making it a favorite for fast-paced and dynamic matches.
